The Best Movies According to Eva

My top 3 movies of all times.

Star Trek IV

The Voyage Home

It's the 23rd century, and a mysterious alien power is threatening Earth by evaporating the oceans and destroying the atmosphere. In a frantic attempt to save mankind, Kirk and his crew must time travel back to 1986 San Francisco where they find a world of punk, pizza and exact-change buses that are as alien as anything they've ever encountered in the far reaches of the galaxy.

StarGate

A historical expert and a military unit travel through a mysterious portal. They land in a bizarre world occupied by a dangerous and powerful being.

The Wackiest Ship in the Army

During WW II, Lt. Rip Crandall and his inexperienced and motley crew get assigned to deliver an Australian observer to a Japanese-held island.
